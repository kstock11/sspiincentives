Survey Incentives

There are two types of questions which affect your bonus - each type has its own scoring rule.
Question type Points earned
Guess the correct number. Higher of (100 - (guess - true value)²) and 0 points.
Guess the correct multiple choice answer. 100 points for correct guess, 0 otherwise.
Note that the rule for questions asking for numerical guesses give points for any guess within 9 percentage points of the true value.
Your probability of winning the bonus is equal to your average score for every question which affects the bonus odds divided by 400.
